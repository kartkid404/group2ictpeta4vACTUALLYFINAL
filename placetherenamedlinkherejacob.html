Gabay sa Lahat ng Kausapang Kontra Droga!

"BKD in MakSci!"

BKD is not only active in other schools but is also currently active in our campus, The Makati Science Highschool, and serves as their "kontra-droga" organization for a very long time now. The BKD has hosted programs, debates about the use of diffrent drugs, and even competitions involving dance to promote the idea of "anti-drugs" to the alma matter.

"BKD in Dance!"

One of the BKD's most recent programs was the dance competition, wherein contestants wil record themselves dancing the given choreography to the given music that implements oneness agaisnt drugs.

"BKD in Red Ribbon Month!"

In celebration for red ribbon month, the BKD of MakSci used this oppurtunity to promote drug prevention, increase awareness of drug addiction, and unity in the fight agaist drugs!
